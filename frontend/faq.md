## üìã General Questions

### What is Jobnests?
Jobnests is a job portal platform that connects job seekers with employers. Job seekers can search and apply for jobs, while employers can post job listings and manage applications.

### Is Jobnests free to use?
Yes! Job seekers can create accounts, search for jobs, and apply for positions completely free. Employers can also post job listings on the platform.

### How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team through this chatbot, or email us directly. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

## üë§ Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
1. Click on the "Register" button on the homepage
2. Choose whether you're a **Job Seeker** or **Employer**
3. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

### What's the difference between a Job Seeker and Employer account?
- **Job Seeker**: Search for jobs, apply to positions, take skill assessments, upload resumes
- **Employer**: Post job listings, review applications, manage candidates, view applicant profiles

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. Go to the Login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Create a new password

### Can I change my account type?
No, you cannot switch between Job Seeker and Employer accounts. You'll need to create a new account with a different email address.

### How do I update my profile?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Profile" from the dashboard menu
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Update your information
5. Click "Save Changes"

## üîç Job Seekers - Searching for Jobs

### How do I search for jobs?
1. Log into your Job Seeker account
2. Go to "Browse Jobs" or use the search bar
3. Enter keywords, job titles, or company names
4. Use filters to narrow results (location, job type, salary range)
5. Click on a job to view details

### What filters are available for job search?
- **Location**: City, state, or remote
- **Job Type**: Full-time, part-time, contract, internship
- **Experience Level**: Entry, mid, senior level
- **Salary Range**: Minimum and maximum salary
- **Date Posted**: Recent listings

### How do I save jobs for later?
Click the "Save" or bookmark icon on any job listing. Access saved jobs from your dashboard under "Saved Jobs."

### Can I set up job alerts?
Yes! Go to your profile settings and configure job alerts based on:
- Keywords
- Job titles
- Locations
- Job types

You'll receive email notifications when matching jobs are posted.

## üìù Job Seekers - Applying for Jobs

### How do I apply for a job?
1. Find a job you're interested in
2. Click "Apply Now"
3. Review your profile information
4. Upload or select your resume
5. Add a cover letter (optional but recommended)
6. Submit your application

### What file formats are accepted for resumes?
We accept:
- PDF (.pdf) - Recommended
- Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
- Maximum file size: 5MB

### Can I edit my application after submitting?
No, applications cannot be edited after submission. Make sure to review all information before clicking submit.

### How do I track my applications?
1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click "My Applications"
3. View the status of each application:
   - **Pending**: Application received, awaiting review
   - **Under Review**: Employer is reviewing your application
   - **Shortlisted**: You've been selected for the next round
   - **Interview**: Interview scheduled
   - **Accepted**: Congratulations! You got the job
   - **Rejected**: Application was not successful

### Why was my application rejected?
Common reasons include:
- Missing qualifications or experience
- Incomplete application
- Position was filled
- Better-matched candidates

Don't get discouraged! Keep applying to other positions.

## üìä Job Seekers - Assessments & Quizzes

### What are skill assessments?
Skill assessments are quizzes that test your knowledge in specific areas. Completing them can:
- Showcase your skills to employers
- Earn badges for your profile
- Increase visibility to recruiters

### How do I take an assessment?
1. Go to "Assessments" from your dashboard
2. Browse available quizzes by category
3. Select a quiz and click "Start"
4. Answer all questions within the time limit
5. View your results and badge (if earned)

### Can I retake an assessment?
Yes, you can retake assessments after a waiting period (usually 7 days). Your best score will be displayed on your profile.

### Are assessments timed?
Yes, most assessments have a time limit. The time remaining is displayed during the quiz.

## üè¢ Employers - Posting Jobs

### How do I post a job?
1. Log into your Employer account
2. Go to "Post a Job" or "Create Job"
3. Fill in job details:
   - Job title
   - Description
   - Requirements
   - Location
   - Salary range
   - Job type
4. Review and publish

### How long do job postings stay active?
Job postings remain active for 30 days by default. You can:
- Extend the posting
- Close it early when the position is filled
- Repost expired listings

### Can I edit a job posting after publishing?
Yes! Go to "My Jobs," click on the listing, and select "Edit." Changes will be reflected immediately.

### How do I close a job posting?
1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Find the listing
3. Click "Close Position" or "Mark as Filled"
4. Confirm closure

## üè¢ Employers - Managing Applications

### How do I view applications?
1. Go to your Employer Dashboard
2. Click "Applications" or select a specific job
3. View all applicants with their:
   - Name and contact info
   - Resume
   - Cover letter
   - Application date
   - Quiz scores (if applicable)

### How do I filter applications?
Use filters to sort by:
- Application date
- Quiz scores
- Experience level
- Status (pending, reviewed, shortlisted)

### How do I contact an applicant?
1. Open the applicant's profile
2. Click "Message" to use in-app messaging
3. Or use their provided email/phone number

### How do I shortlist candidates?
1. Open the application
2. Click "Shortlist" or change status to "Shortlisted"
3. The candidate will be notified

## üí¨ Messaging

### How do I send messages?
1. Go to "Messages" from your dashboard
2. Click "New Message" or select an existing conversation
3. Type your message and click Send

### Who can I message?
- **Job Seekers**: Can message employers for jobs they've applied to
- **Employers**: Can message any applicant

### Are messages private?
Yes, all messages are private between you and the recipient. We do not share message content.

### I'm not receiving message notifications
Check your:
1. Email notification settings in your profile
2. Spam/junk folder
3. Browser notification permissions

## üîí Privacy & Security

### Is my information secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your personal information is never shared without consent.

### Who can see my profile?
- **Job Seekers**: Employers can view your profile when you apply for jobs
- **Employers**: Your company profile is public to job seekers

### How do I delete my account?
1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Scroll to "Account"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### How do I download my data?
Go to Profile Settings > Privacy > "Download My Data" to receive a copy of all your information.

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### I can't log in
Try these steps:
1. Check your email and password are correct
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Reset your password
5. Contact support if issues persist

### The page isn't loading
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Disable browser extensions

### I'm not receiving emails
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add our email to your contacts
3. Verify your email address is correct in settings
4. Check email notification preferences

### My resume won't upload
1. Check file format (PDF or DOC/DOCX)
2. Ensure file is under 5MB
3. Try a different browser
4. Rename file without special characters
